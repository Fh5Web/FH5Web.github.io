Turn 10 Studios

Turn 10 Studios is a video game developer based in Redmond, Washington. It was founded in 2001 and is a subsidiary of Microsoft Studios. The studio is primarily known for developing the Forza Motorsport series, a racing game franchise that has been praised for its realism and attention to detail. The studio has also developed other racing games, including the spin-off Forza Horizon series and the mobile game Forza Street. Turn 10 Studios has received critical acclaim for its games and has won numerous awards, including multiple "Racing Game of the Year" awards. The studio has also been recognized for its contributions to the gaming industry, particularly in the area of simulation and realism in racing games.

Playground Games

Playground Games is a British video game developer based in Leamington Spa, England. The studio was founded in 2010 and is a subsidiary of Xbox Game Studios. Playground Games is best known for developing the Forza Horizon series, a spin-off of the Forza Motorsport franchise, which features open-world racing and exploration. The studio has also been involved in the development of the Fable series, a popular role-playing game franchise. Playground Games has received critical acclaim for its games and has won multiple awards, including "Best Racing Game" and "Best British Game" at the BAFTA Games Awards. The studio has also been recognized for its commitment to diversity and inclusivity in the gaming industry.
